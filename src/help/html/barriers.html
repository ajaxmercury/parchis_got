Barriers in the Game

If a player choose in a step to place one chip in a box already occupied by another chip with the same color, this will be a barrier.
Barriers make impossible the step of any chip toward the goal (even chips of the owner of the barrier).

If a chip would not complete the step by the total number of boxes that correspond, then the movement would not be made.
When a player have a barrier by some of their chips, and in his turn he throw a 6, he would be enforce to open the barrier, it means to move one of the chip that take part of the barrier.
